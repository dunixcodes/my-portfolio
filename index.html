About Me

Hello, thank you for visiting my website. Yes -- my name is Unix, though I was not really named after the operating system.

I have always been fascinated with computers and technology but it never entered my mind to pursue a career in information technology. It was not until I worked as an IT asset manager that I started to seriously consider the idea. I was working side-by-side with IT folks and it really motivated me because I understood the work they were doing and I picked up on concepts pretty quickly.

I started applying for IT jobs and it was difficult to be taken seriously since I did not have a formal education or experience. I mostly learned either by looking things up on the internet, taking courses online, or from experimenting on my personal computer that I built from scratch. I was adviced to take the CompTIA Security+ by one of the recruiters and so I dedicated 2 months to study for the certification. I got it on the first try and I was ecstatic!

That was my start in IT and now that I have gained more experience, I realized that the cloud is the next big thing in the industry. I decided to create this portfolio to gain practical skills in cloud technologies and showcase what I have learned so far.

I am familiar with basic programming concepts such as OOP, and algorithmic design from learning Python.

Other than working on my portfolio, I am also currently pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Software development and Security.

I hope to be part of a DevSecOpsnteam where I can use my broad knowledge of system administration, information security, cloud technologies, and software development to find efficient solutions for today's problems.
